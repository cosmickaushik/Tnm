Rules and Guidelines

1. One entry per college.
2. It is a team event. A team can have 15-18 members. (Including participants, choreographer and coordinator).
3. Time limit for every team would be 15 minutes (Including setup and the performance both).
4. Negative marking if participants exceed time limit.
5. Theme selection is open to the team.
6. A green room would be provided for the changing purpose.
7. Should carry their tracks in pendrive or Aux.
8. Vulgarity is strongly prohibited. Any form of obscenity will lead to debarring the team from the contest.
9 Use of cigarettes, alcohol and any unfair means is strongly prohibited.


Basis of Judgement

Teams will be judged on costumes, theme, walking stance and attitude. Decision of the judges will be final and binding.

Rules and Guidelines

1. There is a preliminary round in which every team has to perform.
2. After the preliminary round, 12 teams are shortlisted for the final round.
3. There are 3 podium positions, an award for best music and people's choice award.
4. There is no team limit and time limit, both for the preliminary round and the final round.
5. Use of electronic equipment is not allowed.
6. There should be no use of derogatory or obscene language targeting any community, gender or religion.
7. Teams must bring their own props.
8. Judge's decisions will be final and abiding.

Judgement Criteria

Teams are judged on the basis of their:
1. Use of props
2. Voice
3. Music
4. Justice to Topic
5. Script
6. Energy
7. Acting

Rules and Guidelines

1. There will be a preliminary online round, entries of which will be accepted via YouTube/Google Drive links.
2. A total time limit of 15 mins per band will be allotted, including the sound check.
3. Using any pre-recorded piece of music is strictly prohibited.
4. Only a standard acoustic drum kit will be provided, including the mics.


Judgement Criteria and Slots

1. Slots will be awarded on first come first serve basis.
2. Use of inappropriate/abusive words is prohibited.
3. Original composition will fetch you extra marks.
4. Teams will be judged upon their composition, co-ordination, fun factor, and stage presence.
5. Marks will be deducted on exceeding time limit.
6. Decision of judges at both the rounds will be final and binding.
