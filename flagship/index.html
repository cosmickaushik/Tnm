The Music Event

Ever played a 7 by 11 on 7/11 at 7:11 outside a 7-11? Well if you understood that, you are not new to the world of music, and that's exactly what we're looking for. If the chords of your soul can rock and roll, and your band can make the audience head bang - then you wouldn't want to miss out on this.Learn More


EVENT GUIDLINES

  1. There will be a preliminary online round, entries of which will be accepted via YouTube/Google Drive links.

  2. A total time limit of 15 mins per band will be allotted, including the sound check.

  3. Using any pre-recorded piece of music is strictly prohibited.

  4. Only a standard acoustic drum kit will be provided, including the mics.

JUDGING CRIETERIA

  1. Slots will be awarded on first come first serve basis.

  2. Use of inappropriate/abusive words is prohibited.

  3. Original composition will fetch you extra marks.

  4. Teams will be judged upon their composition, co-ordination, fun factor, and stage presence.

  5. Marks will be deducted on exceeding time limit.

  6. Decision of judges at both the rounds will be final and binding.

Run Through

Drama Event

Runthrough is the Annual Street Play event organized by Aayaam-The Dramatics Society of MAIT. Teams from all over the Delhi Collegiate Theatre Circuit participate in it. It is a platform where talented theater enthusiasts showcase their talent and get their voices heard on various social awareness topics, ranging from, Drug Abuse to Environmental Pollution. Towards the end of the final round, Aayaam releases its Mashup of the Year, which is amalgamation of songs composed by the teams during that specific yearLearn More


EVENT GUIDLINES

  1. There is a preliminary round in which every team has to perform.

  2. After the preliminary round, 12 teams are shortlisted for the final round.

  3. The time limit of each team will be 25 minutes. The total time will be from empty stage to empty stage.

  4. There are 3 podium positions, an award for best music and people's choice award.

  5. There is no team limit and time limit, both for the preliminary round and the final round.

EVENT RULES

  1. Use of electronic equipment is not allowed.

  2. There should be no use of derogatory or obscene language targeting any community, gender or religion.

  3. Teams must bring their own props.

  4. Judge's decisions will be final and abiding.

JUDGING CRIETERIA

  1. Use of props

  2. Voice

  3. Music

  4. Justice to Topic

  5. Script

  6. Energy

  7. Acting
